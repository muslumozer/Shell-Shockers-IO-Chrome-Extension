Special Tactics (Only in this addon)

These tactics are exclusive to this chrome extension only. Legal rights will be used in case of unauthorized use on any site.

* Do not act separately from your teammates. If you are close to each other, you can easily defeat an enemy team when they attack you.

* If your gun runs out of ammo during combat, press the E key to switch to the pistol gun instead of pressing the R key and changing the magazine. If you try to reload without taking cover, the enemy may kill you at that time. If you press the E key and fight with the pistol, you will reduce your risk of dying a little bit.

* If you have seen the enemy and you have run out of ammo, press the Q key to throw a cluster bomb at the enemy. This will save you as much time as reloading.

* Focus on prefire while checking corners. This way, if there is an egg, Pusan will take damage. Then you kill him by continuing to shoot bullets at him.

How To play

Shell Shock.io is a 3D egg-based multiplayer tactical online shooter. In the game you fight against other online armed eggs with you, one blue team and one red team, the game mode with 2 teams is more prominent.

Forward : W
Backward : S
Left : A
Right : D
Jump : Space
Fire : Mouse 0
Reload : R
Swap Weapon : E
